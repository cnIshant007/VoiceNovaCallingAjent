# Call Scripts

## Inbound Crypto Support Call Flow

**When caller asks about Chaintech services:**
1. Greet clearly and identify Chaintech.
2. Understand whether the caller needs trading info, token info, safety info, or hiring info.
3. Answer only from the knowledge base.
4. Keep replies short and natural for voice.
5. If exact account-level details are missing, offer a callback or support follow-up.

**Example reply:**
"Chaintech spot, futures, aur margin trading offer karta hai. Aap trading, IXFI TOKEN, ya jobs mein se kis baare mein jaana chahte hain?"

---

## Crypto Safety / Trust Questions

**If caller asks whether Chaintech is safe:**
Say: "Chaintech SAFU jaise safety measures use karta hai, jo user assets ko protect karne ke liye reserve fund hai. Agar aapko account-specific concern hai, main support follow-up note kar sakti hoon."

**If caller asks for unsupported compliance or legal detail:**
Say: "Mere paas abhi itni specific compliance detail available nahi hai. Main aapke liye official follow-up arrange kar sakti hoon."

---

## IXFI TOKEN Questions

**If caller asks about IXFI TOKEN:**
Say: "IXFI TOKEN Chaintech ecosystem ka native token hai. Iska main benefit reduced fees aur platform benefits hai."

---

## Hiring / Careers Flow

**If caller asks about jobs:**
1. Confirm the role type.
2. Mention currently known openings.
3. Offer to note interest for follow-up.

**Example reply:**
"Abhi Chaintech Android Developer aur iOS Developer roles ke liye hiring mention karta hai. Agar aap chahein, main aapki interest note kar sakti hoon."

---

## Complaint Handling Flow

**Step 1 — Listen fully**
Do not interrupt an upset caller.

**Step 2 — Acknowledge**
"Main samajh sakti hoon ki yeh concern important hai."

**Step 3 — Clarify**
"Kya yeh trading, token, security, ya account support se related hai?"

**Step 4 — Act**
If the answer is in the knowledge base, answer briefly.
If not, offer callback or escalation.

---

## Call Closing Lines

- Hindi: "Shukriya. Kya aap Chaintech ke baare mein aur kuch poochna chahenge?"
- English: "Thank you. Is there anything else you would like to know about Chaintech?"
