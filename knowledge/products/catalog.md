# Chaintech Product & Service Catalog

## Crypto Trading Services

### Spot Trading
- Buy and sell supported crypto assets on the spot market
- Designed for users who want direct asset trading

### Futures Trading
- Access futures products for advanced trading strategies
- Suitable for users who understand derivatives risk

### Margin Trading
- Borrowed-capital trading for advanced users
- Users should understand liquidation and leverage risks before using margin

## Asset Coverage
- Supports roughly 350-500 cryptocurrencies
- Known for broad crypto access, deep liquidity, and competitive transaction fees
